The Story Behind Solution Track

I'm Stephen Frahm, founder of
Solution Track Consulting.

My approach to IT is shaped by two decades of high-stakes engineering and a background defined by service and exploration. As a veteran, I built my professional foundation on discipline and a mission-first mindset. In enterprise communications, where a down system or a compliance gap can have real-world consequences, that discipline is what keeps clients operational and protected.

Beyond the data center, I'm an avid world traveler. Navigating diverse cultures and complex international environments has sharpened my ability to communicate across technical and business silos — and to bring that same adaptability to forensic audits, helping teams bridge the gap between legacy technical debt and modern cloud strategy.

With over 25 years in the Cisco UC ecosystem, I've seen that technical failures are rarely the fault of the hardware. They're the result of poor discovery. I started this firm to provide a forensic alternative to the staff-aug status quo — giving enterprise IT teams and global partners a senior-level perspective they can trust.
