Strategic Audit - Operational Health

Stop Firefighting.
Start Running a System.

When your UC team is spending more time reacting than building, the problem is usually architecture. The Operational Health Audit identifies what is driving your reactive cycles and builds a path out of them.

Schedule Your Operational Audit View All Services

Most UC operational problems are not talent problems. They are documentation debt, undocumented change history, and monitoring blind spots that compound over time until every outage feels like a surprise.

Scope of Work

What We Assess

Three areas where configuration drift and technical debt accumulate fastest in mature UC environments.

Change Control Archaeology

We trace every undocumented change in your environment, identify what broke and when, and establish a baseline your team can manage from going forward.

  • Configuration drift mapping across CUCM nodes
  • Change history reconstruction
  • Orphaned device and route pattern inventory
  • Unknown dependency identification

Monitoring and Alerting Gaps

We assess what your environment is and is not telling you, and identify the blind spots that turn small problems into outages.

  • Alarm and alert coverage audit
  • Syslog and CDR gap analysis
  • Database replication health check
  • Resource utilization baseline

Documentation and Runbook Integrity

We assess whether your team has what they need to operate and troubleshoot the environment without relying on institutional knowledge that walks out the door.

  • Runbook completeness review
  • Diagram currency check
  • Escalation path documentation
  • Knowledge transfer gap assessment
Deliverables

What You Walk Away With

Two outputs designed to be immediately actionable by your team, not a consulting artifact that lives in a drawer.

Operational Health Report

A candid, prioritized assessment of where your environment stands, written for engineers, not executives. Every finding ranked by operational risk and remediation complexity.

  • Change control gap register with risk rankings
  • Monitoring coverage heatmap across critical call paths
  • Documentation delta report: what exists vs. what's accurate
  • Orphaned asset inventory ready for decommission review
  • Top 10 reactive-cycle drivers with root cause analysis

90-Day Stabilization Roadmap

A sequenced remediation plan your team can execute without outside help, prioritized by impact, scoped to your available capacity, and designed to compound over time.

  • Week-by-week remediation task sequencing
  • Quick wins (under 4 hours) broken out for immediate execution
  • Monitoring gap closure playbook with tool-specific guidance
  • Documentation rebuild templates pre-populated with your environment
  • 30-day check-in option to review progress and adjust
