Most UC operational problems aren't talent problems — they're documentation debt, undocumented change history, and monitoring blind spots that compound over time until every outage feels like a surprise.

Scope of Work

What We Assess

Three operational dimensions where drift and debt accumulate fastest in mature UC environments.

🔍

Change Control Archaeology

Your environment has been touched by dozens of hands over the years. We reconstruct what was actually changed — and why — so you can stop treating your own system as a black box.

  • Undocumented configuration delta analysis
  • Change history reconstruction from audit logs
  • Orphaned device and user account inventory
  • Shadow IT integrations and rogue gateways
📊

Monitoring and Alerting Gaps

If you're finding out about problems from end users instead of your monitoring stack, your observability posture has eroded. We map what you're watching, what you're missing, and what silence actually means.

  • Alert coverage audit against critical call paths
  • RTMT / CDR / CMR gap identification
  • Synthetic call test coverage assessment
  • SLA-to-monitoring alignment review
📋

Documentation and Runbook Integrity

Documentation that doesn't match the live environment is worse than no documentation — it costs you time during an incident. We verify what exists, what's stale, and what's missing entirely.

  • Network diagram accuracy verification
  • Runbook validation against current config
  • Escalation path and contact freshness review
  • Disaster recovery procedure gap analysis
Deliverables

What You Walk Away With

Two outputs designed to be immediately actionable by your team — not a consulting artifact that lives in a drawer.

📄

Operational Health Report

A candid, prioritized assessment of where your environment stands — written for engineers, not executives. Every finding ranked by operational risk and remediation complexity.

  • Change control gap register with risk rankings
  • Monitoring coverage heatmap across critical call paths
  • Documentation delta report: what exists vs. what's accurate
  • Orphaned asset inventory ready for decommission review
  • Top 10 reactive-cycle drivers with root cause analysis
🗺️

90-Day Stabilization Roadmap

A sequenced remediation plan your team can execute without outside help — prioritized by impact, scoped to your available capacity, and designed to compound over time.

  • Week-by-week remediation task sequencing
  • Quick wins (under 4 hours) broken out for immediate execution
  • Monitoring gap closure playbook with tool-specific guidance
  • Documentation rebuild templates pre-populated with your environment
  • 30-day check-in option to review progress and adjust
